Our Story

Rooted in East African Tradition

Savanna Grill was born from a simple belief — that food is the most honest expression of culture. Our head chef, Ibrahim Waweru, trained in Addis Ababa, Zanzibar, and Johannesburg before bringing his craft home to Nairobi.

Every dish on our menu is built around ingredients sourced within 100km of our kitchen. We work directly with farmers in Limuru, fishermen on the coast, and spice traders in Gikomba.
